The playing site

The tournament is taking place in the congress hall of the Hotel Duo, Teplická 9, Prague 9, (see it on Google Maps). The hotel is located in the northern part of Prague, next to the highway to Teplice/Dresden.

Accomodation

We really recommend players to accommodate in the Hotel Duo (playing site), which has a high standard (4 stars) and is inexpensive; the tournament offer is very nice (see table below). It is very convenient to be staying directly at playing site. For those who want to explore the city center, note that Red Metro C (underground) which is 5 mins from the hotel is in city center in 12 minutes (6 stops).

Please, do not hesitate to Contact us if you needed any assistance.

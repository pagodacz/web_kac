The playing site

The tournament is taking place in two hotels of the Olympik complex at U Sluncové 14A, Prague 8:

  • Hotel Olympik Congress**** - main venue
  • Hotel Olympik Tristar**** - secondary venue
The hotels are located in Prague's Karlín district. The venue previously hosted the European Youth Go Championship 2022.

Public Transport: Metro B station Invalidovna, just 5 minutes (3 stops) from the city center.

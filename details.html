Tournament Details

Date 2–4 May 2025, see the Schedule on the right.
Site and Accommodation See the dedicated page.
Rules
System
  • 6 rounds, MacMahon, SOS
Rules Japanese rules, 6.5 komi
Time Fisher Time, 45 minutes + 20 sec / move
Miscellaneous
Grand Prix The tournament takes part in European Grand Prix 2024, as a Bonus Point Level-B tournament.
Organization
  • Tournament director: Vladimír Daněk
  • Chief referee: Aleš Cieplý
Side Tournament Apart from the main event, we will also hold a small side tournament for children and fast players; it will be organized by Jana Hricová.
Usage of electronics To ensure fair-play, usage of all electronic devices is forbidden during games. Paper kifu will be available.

Prizes

Total prize budget over 10 000 EUR
1st–10th place 700, 550, 450, 400, 350, 300, 250, 200, 150, 100 EUR.
Top 5 EGF players 600, 500, 400, 300, 200 EUR.
Cumulative with prizes for 1st–10th place.
Special prizes:
Best players in their class (low kyu, high kyu, 1.dan, TBD) 50 EUR
Best Woman 100 EUR
6 & 5 & 4 points 80 EUR, 50 EUR, 20 EUR
Top young players in categories — U12, U16, U20 Valuable material prices, or prize money.
Prizes are not cumulative (unless written otherwise). In case of player winning more prizes, only the bigger one counts. For example, player winning the best woman while having 4 points only gets the best woman prize.
